Truong-Son Van
sonv@andrew.cmu.edu Department of Mathematical Sciences, Carnegie Mellon University

Starting July 2021, I will be a Hans Rademacher Instructor in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Pennsylvania. Prior to Fall 2021, I was a student of Gautam Iyer and Bob Pego at Carnegie Mellon University.

My current research interest lies in some intersection between partial differential equations and probability. I am keen on proving theorems about phenomena that come from God’s beautiful world and I thank Him everyday that I can do it with peace and joy. These days, I spend a lot of time thinking about the advection-diffusion equation, the coagulation-fragmentation equation, and the Hamilton-Jacobi equation.
