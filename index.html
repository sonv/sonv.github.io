Truong-Son Van
I am a thinker. I was a Hans Rademacher Instructor in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Pennsylvania from 2021-2022. Prior to Fall 2021, I was a student of Gautam Iyer and Bob Pego at Carnegie Mellon University.
