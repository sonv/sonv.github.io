Turning your camera to a lightboard using OBS

2020-08-30

Teaching online has become a thing due to COVID-19. During the Summer of 2020, I created a documentation on how to build a Lightboard here.

There is also a version of the Lightboard using OBS and any writing device that could be broadcast to the computer (ipad, android, surface, etc.) by playing around with chroma key and green screen. This is not only great for teaching but also good for online seminar presentations as the visual effect is usually much better than what meeting platforms can offer.

Software for Ubuntu:

  1. OBS
  2. uxPlay

Software for Windows:

  1. OBS
  2. Reflector

Software for Mac:

  1. OBS
  2. Either UBS connection with Quicktime or Reflector
